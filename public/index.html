Frequently Asked Questions

Is your service completely confidential?

Yes, we operate with strict confidentiality protocols. All client information is encrypted and stored securely. Anonymous services are available for clients who require an additional layer of privacy.

How does the contract process work?

You submit a contract request with details about your case. Our team reviews it and either approves or rejects based on feasibility and legal considerations. If approved, 50% payment is required upfront before work begins.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept PayPal and various cryptocurrencies (Bitcoin, Ethereum, etc.) to provide flexibility and additional anonymity options for our clients.

How long does an investigation typically take?

Timelines vary based on case complexity. Simple cases may be resolved in 2-3 days, while more complex investigations can take several weeks. We provide estimated timelines during contract approval.

Is your work legal?

Yes, all our investigative methods comply with applicable laws and regulations. We only use legal OSINT techniques and publicly available information sources.

Can I remain completely anonymous?

Yes, we offer fully anonymous services where you can use an alias and communicate through encrypted channels. Payment via cryptocurrency enhances anonymity.
